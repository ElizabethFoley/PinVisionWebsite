Welcome to PinVision Pizza and Italian Restaurant. We have been dedicated to serving the Poughkeepsie area the highest quality cuisine for over 25 years. Established by the Vision brothers in 1994, every dish is a journey straight from their home in Italy to yours. Come in and enjoy our wide selection of pizza, pasta, salads, calzones, and more. We use only the finest ingredients and all our food is hand made to order by our professional chefs. We are certain you will leave full and satisfied!
